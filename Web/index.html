Types of disorder

Here are brief descriptions of some mental health disorders:

  • Anxiety disorders
  • Mood Disorders
  • Post-traumatic stress disorder
  • Obsessive compulsive disorder
  • Personality disorders

Anxiety disorders

Anxiety disorders are a cluster of mental disorders characterized by significant and uncontrollable feelings of anxiety and fear such that a person's social, occupational, and personal function are significantly impaired.

Mood Disorders

A mood disorder is a mental health condition that primarily affects your emotional state. It's a disorder in which you experience long periods of extreme happiness, extreme sadness or both. Certain mood disorders involve other persistent emotions, such as anger and irritability

Post-traumatic stress disorder

Post-traumatic stress disorder is a mental and behavioral disorder that develops from experiencing a traumatic event, such as sexual assault, warfare, traffic collisions, child abuse, domestic violence, or other threats on a person's life or well-being.

Obsessive compulsive disorder

Obsessive compulsive disorder is a mental and behavioral disorder in which an individual has intrusive thoughts and feels the need to perform certain routines repeatedly to relieve the distress caused by the obsession, to the extent where it impairs general function.

Personality disorders

Personality disorders are a class of mental disorders characterized by enduring maladaptive patterns of behavior, cognition, and inner experience, exhibited across many contexts and deviating from those accepted by the individual's culture.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the frequently asked questions (FAQs) that users commonly inquire about our app and the services it provides.

  • How does Baymax, the chatbot, help in identifying mental health issues?

    Baymax, our chatbot, aids in identifying mental health issues through a series of user-friendly assessments. These assessments are designed to evaluate various aspects of mental well-being, such as mood, anxiety, and stress levels. By analyzing your responses, Baymax can provide initial insights into your mental health status.

  • Can I trust the confidentiality of my interactions with the chatbot?

    We take the confidentiality of your interactions with Baymax seriously. Your privacy is a top priority. All conversations and data within the app are encrypted to ensure that your personal information and discussions remain secure and private.

  • What screening tools and assessments are integrated into the app to evaluate my mental health?

    Our app integrates several screening tools and assessments, including the Patient Health Questionnaire (PHQ-9), Generalized Anxiety Disorder 7 (GAD-7), and Columbia-Suicide Severity Rating Scale (C-SSRS). These tools help in early detection and categorization of mental health conditions, providing valuable information for both users and healthcare professionals.

  • How can I access sound therapy through the app for stress relief?

    To help you manage stress, our app offers scientifically proven sound therapy sessions. These sessions utilize soothing sounds and music to promote relaxation and reduce stress levels. You can access these sessions within the app to help you unwind and find peace.

  • Are there any emergency support features available in case I require immediate assistance?

    In case of emergencies or if you need immediate assistance, our app connects you with on-call doctors and mental health professionals who can provide timely support and guidance. Your well-being is our priority, and we're here to assist you whenever you need it.
